Our Story

When we moved to Canada, we searched everywhere for the authentic taste of Punjabi achar that reminded us of home. The pickles available here just couldn't match the bold, complex flavors we grew up with in Punjab.

That's when we decided to bring our family's generations-old recipe to Canada. Passed down through four generations, our recipe uses traditional slow-cooking methods and authentic spices sourced directly from Punjab.

Every jar of Zaildaar Achar is handcrafted in small batches using premium Canadian meat and traditional Punjabi techniques. We're not just selling pickle – we're sharing a piece of our heritage and bringing that missing taste of home to Punjabi families across Canada.

Our mission is simple: to deliver the exact authentic flavors of Punjab to your Canadian kitchen.
